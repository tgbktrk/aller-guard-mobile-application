About About AllerGuard With AllerGuard, product allergen information can be accessed by scanning the product barcode. If the user has added their own allergens, the system warns them if there is a common allergen when the product barcode is scanned. You can view and edit allergen information on the My Allergens page. Features
  • Barcode Scanning: Easily scan product barcodes to instantly access allergen information, eliminating the need to read labels manually.
  • Allergen Warnings: Receive real-time alerts if a scanned product contains allergens that could harm you based on your saved allergen profile.
  • Personalized Allergen Management: Save your personal allergen information in the app and get customized recommendations for products to avoid or consider.
  • Prevention of Anaphylactic Reactions: The app helps prevent potential anaphylactic reactions by warning you about harmful products before purchase.
  • Detailed Allergen Information: Access comprehensive details about various allergens, helping you make informed decisions.
 How to Use
  1. Create Personal Profile: Enter your personal information when you first open the app.
  2. Add Allergens: Add the allergens you have.
  3. Scan Barcode: Scan the barcode of the product you want to buy.
  4. Get Alert: If there is a warning message, that product contains the allergy you have. So you should be careful when buying it.
